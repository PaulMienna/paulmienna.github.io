*The surface temperature on Venus is hot, reaching around 467 degrees Celsius, making it the hottest planet in our solar system.

*A day on Venus (one full rotation) takes longer than a year; it rotates once roughly every 243 days.

*It only takes 225 days to rotate around the sun, making its year shorter than its day.

*Lacks a moon

Facts

* Venus is the second planet from the Sun in our solar system.
* It is referred to as Earth's "sister planet" due to similarities in size and mass.

* Venus has a diameter of approximately 12,104 kilometers, making it slightly smaller than Earth. Making its mass roughly 86% of Earths.

* One of the brightest objects in the night sky after sunset.
